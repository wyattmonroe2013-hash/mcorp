Administrative Account System

Restricted Monroe Corporation access.

New administrative accounts must first verify through an Emerald Games account with role: admin. After registration, administrators sign in with their separate Monroe Corporation administrative account.

Access Process

Register once. Sign in with Monroe credentials afterward.

01

Verify Emerald Games admin status

The registration form checks the Emerald Games users collection and requires the account to have role: "admin".

02

Create the Monroe administrative account

The site stores a new record in the administrativeAccounts collection with its own username and SHA-256 password hash.

03

Use the administrative account for login

Future access uses the Monroe Corporation administrative account instead of the Emerald Games password.
